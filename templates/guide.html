Guide

You have to format the problems in a specific way, or you will get an internal server error. Here is an example: Section: 4-6, Pages: 287-293, Problems: 8, 9, 10, 11-16, 20-26 even, 31-37 odd, 10-25 every 5
This text will be put at the top of each page and will be parsed to get the problem numbers. The only thing that you have to include is the "Problems: x, y, z" at the end. The generator also allows for variations of numbers:

  • Single number: x, y, z
  • Number range: x-y
  • Odd range: x-y odd
  • Even range: x-y even
  • Multiples x-y every z

Enjoy!
